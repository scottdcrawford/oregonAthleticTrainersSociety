Job Posting Policy

OATS Job Posting Policy

One of the objectives of OATS is “The advancement, encouragement, and improvement of the athletic training profession in all its phases.” OATS encourages dissemination of athletic training position announcements to OATS members. If you would like a position announcement sent to OATS members please follow the subsequent steps. Any questions can be directed to the OATS President Heather Halseth.

Permanent Positions:

1. The position must first be posted on the NATA Career Center. There are a variety of posting options available so make sure you select the most appropriate option. Additionally, secondary school AT positions may be eligible to post on the NATA Career Center for a discounted rate.

2. After the position is posted on the NATA Career Center please contact the OATS President Heather Halseth to have the position announcement sent to the OATS membership. Include in this email the blurb / details of the position including your contact information that you would like shared with the OATS members. Also please utilize correct terminology in your announcement, such as Athletic Trainer or AT instead of Trainer.

3. OATS will then send out the position announcement.

Independent Contractor / Per Diem Positions:

1. OATS will send out per diem AT positions to the OATS membership if the position pays a minimum of $25/hour*.

2. To have the position announcement sent to the OATS membership send your position announcement to the OATS President Heather Halseth. Include in this email the blurb / details of the position including:

  1. date(s) and times
  2. whether supplies will be provided, and
  3. your contact information.

Also please utilize correct terminology in your announcement, such as Athletic Trainer or AT instead of Trainer.

3. OATS will then send out the position announcement.

* New Minimum Wage

Effective 09/01/2019, OATS will send out requests for an independent contractor / per diem athletic trainer that meet the following wage:

  1. If the event provides professional liability insurance covering the athletic trainer, the minimum wage is $27.50 / hour
  2. If the event does not provide professional liability insurance for the athletic trainer, the wage is $30.00 / hour

Effective 09/01/2021, the minimum wage will be:

  1. If the event provides professional liability insurance covering the athletic trainer, the minimum wage is $30.00 / hour
  2. If the event does not provide professional liability insurance for the athletic trainer, the wage is $32.50 / hour
