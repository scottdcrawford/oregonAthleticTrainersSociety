OATS Summer Symposium

Summer Symposium Information

The OATS Summer Symposium with be held on the University of Oregon Campus. Friday's event will be at the Marcus Mariota Performance Center (Map) on the U of O campus. Saturday's event will be held at the Erb Memorial Union in Redwood Auditorium (Map) on the U of O campus. AT's can earn up to 8.5 CEU's for both days with two of those credits being EBP's (pending).

Before May 24th the cost for Friday only is $20 for members and $35 for non-members. Saturday only is $65 for member and $80 for non-members. If you would like to attend both days it's $80 for members and $95 for non-members. Students may attend at a reduced cost. A late charge of $10 will be added after 05/24/2018. Breakfast and Lunch are included with registration for Saturday. If you need to pay by check or in cash please contact Tony Walther at tonywalther@pacificu.edu to ensure you receive the early registration cost. Click here if you would like a copy of the schedule.
