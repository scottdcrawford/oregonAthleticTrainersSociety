OATS Summer Symposium

Summer Symposium Information

The OATS Summer Symposium with be held at OHSU School of Nursing on Saturday June 8th. AT's can earn CEU's for attending the event.



The cost to attend the event is:

Members: $75

Non-Members: $90

Student Members: $20

Student Non-Members: $30

ON-SITE registration (day of event): Additional $15

If you need to pay by check or in cash please contact Tony Walther at tonywalther@pacificu.edu to ensure you receive the early registration cost. Click here if you would like a copy of the schedule.
