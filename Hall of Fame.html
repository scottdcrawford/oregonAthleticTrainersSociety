NATA’s Most Distinguished Athletic Trainer

Please congratulate OATS members Mark Hoffman and Tara Lepp who will receive the NATA’s Most Distinguished Athletic Trainer award at the NATA Annual Meeting in June 2013 in Las Vegas. The Most Distinguished Athletic Trainer award recognizes NATA members who have demonstrated exceptional commitment to leadership, volunteer service, advocacy and distinguished professional activities as an athletic trainer. Both of these individuals have been strong advocates and representatives of our profession and our professional organizations and are very, very deserving of this award. Tara and Mark, thank you for all that you have done and continue to do on our behalf!
